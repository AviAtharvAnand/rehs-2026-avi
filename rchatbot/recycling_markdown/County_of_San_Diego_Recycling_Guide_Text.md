# County of San Diego Recycling Guide

# RECYCLE THAT!

## PAPER

### Accepted

-   Newspapers
-   Magazines
-   Junk Mail & Office Paper
-   Flattened Cardboard
-   Paper Containers
-   Milk, Juice & Soup Cartons

### Not Accepted

-   Tissues/Napkins

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## PLASTIC

### Accepted

-   Plastic Bottles
-   Plastic Cups
-   Plastic Tubs
-   Plastic Containers

### Not Accepted

-   Plastic Bags

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## GLASS

### Accepted

-   Glass Bottles and Jars (Colored & Clear)

### Not Accepted

-   Windows
-   Ceramics
-   Drinking Glasses

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## METAL

### Accepted

-   Steel Bottles
-   Steel Cans
-   Aluminum Bottles
-   Aluminum Cans
-   Empty Aerosol Cans

### Not Accepted

-   Paint Cans (unless empty and dry)
