# Master Recycling Guide

## Part 8 --- Frequently Asked Questions & Edge Cases

### Overview

This section answers common "Can I recycle...?" questions. Because
recycling rules vary, answers use terms like **usually**, **often**, or
**check locally** when acceptance differs by community.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Paper & Cardboard

### Can I recycle a pizza box?

Usually yes if clean. Remove greasy portions and compost or discard
them.

### Can I recycle receipts?

Thermal receipts are usually not recyclable.

### Can I recycle wrapping paper?

Plain paper: usually yes. Foil, glitter, or plastic-coated wrapping
paper: usually no.

### Can I recycle padded envelopes?

Usually no in curbside recycling.

### Can I recycle shredded paper?

Sometimes. Many programs require it to be bagged in a paper bag.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Plastics

### Can I recycle a water bottle?

Usually yes after emptying and rinsing.

### Can I recycle a yogurt tub?

Often yes.

### Can I recycle a peanut butter jar?

Usually yes after cleaning.

### Can I recycle plastic utensils?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle plastic straws?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle grocery bags?

Usually no curbside. Use participating retail drop-off programs.

### Can I recycle bubble wrap?

Usually through store drop-off, not curbside.

### Can I recycle Styrofoam?

Most curbside programs do not accept it.

### Can I recycle a toothbrush?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle a toothbrush tube?

Often yes if it is a rigid plastic container.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Glass

### Can I recycle wine bottles?

Usually yes.

### Can I recycle mason jars?

Usually yes.

### Can I recycle mirrors?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle drinking glasses?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle Pyrex?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle broken glass?

Follow local guidance; many programs do not accept it curbside.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Metal

### Can I recycle aluminum foil?

Usually yes if clean.

### Can I recycle foil trays?

Usually yes if clean.

### Can I recycle soup cans?

Yes after emptying and rinsing.

### Can I recycle aerosol cans?

Many programs accept completely empty cans.

### Can I recycle propane cylinders?

No. Use specialty collection.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Organics

### Can I compost coffee grounds?

Usually yes.

### Can I compost eggshells?

Usually yes.

### Can I compost meat?

Many municipal compost programs accept meat; many backyard compost
systems do not.

### Can I compost tea bags?

Only if they do not contain plastic.

### Can I compost paper towels?

Often yes when used with food or water only.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Electronics & Hazardous Waste

### Can I recycle batteries?

Not in curbside recycling. Use battery collection programs.

### Can I recycle laptops?

Use certified e-waste recycling.

### Can I recycle televisions?

Use electronics recycling.

### Can I recycle paint?

Oil-based paint requires HHW disposal. Latex paint rules vary.

### Can I recycle motor oil?

Use automotive or HHW collection.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Clothing & Household Items

### Can I recycle clothes?

Donate or use textile recycling.

### Can I recycle shoes?

Donate usable pairs or use shoe recycling.

### Can I recycle mattresses?

Use mattress recycling where available.

### Can I recycle furniture?

Donate if usable or schedule bulky-item pickup.

### Can I recycle carpets?

Use specialty carpet recycling where available.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Holiday & Miscellaneous Items

### Can I recycle Christmas lights?

Usually through seasonal electronics recycling.

### Can I recycle artificial trees?

Usually no curbside.

### Can I recycle natural Christmas trees?

Many communities offer seasonal composting.

### Can I recycle balloons?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle diapers?

Usually no.

### Can I recycle pet waste?

Usually no in recycling. Compost only where specifically allowed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# When the Answer Is Unclear

If you cannot determine whether an item is recyclable:

1.  Check your city or county recycling website.
2.  Contact your waste hauler.
3.  Search a trusted recycling directory.
4.  Avoid wish-cycling.
