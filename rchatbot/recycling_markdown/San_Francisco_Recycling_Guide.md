# San Francisco Recycling Guide

> **Note:** San Francisco requires residents and businesses to
> participate in recycling and composting programs.

## Recycling (Blue Bin)

Place these items in the blue recycling bin:

-   Paper
-   Clean paper
-   Cardboard
-   Plastic bottles and containers
-   Glass bottles and jars
-   Aluminum cans
-   Steel and metal cans

Do **not** place:

-   Plastic bags
-   Food scraps
-   Yard waste
-   Hazardous waste
-   Electronics

## Compost (Green Bin)

Place these items in the green compost bin:

-   Food scraps
-   Soiled paper
-   Plants
-   Yard trimmings

Do not place:

-   Plastic bags
-   Glass
-   Metal
-   Electronics

## Landfill (Black Bin)

Use the landfill bin only for items that cannot be recycled or
composted.

Examples include: - Non-recyclable mixed materials - Certain hygiene
products - Other non-recoverable waste

## Special Items

Do not place these in any curbside bin:

-   Batteries
-   Paint
-   Chemicals
-   Electronics
-   Large bulky items

Contact Recology or SF Recycles for proper disposal and reuse options.

## Recycling Tips

-   Empty containers.
-   Keep recyclables clean.
-   Place materials loose in the bin.
-   Never bag recyclables in plastic bags.
-   Compost food scraps whenever possible.
