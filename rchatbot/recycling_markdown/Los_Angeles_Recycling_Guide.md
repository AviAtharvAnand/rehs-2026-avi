# Los Angeles Recycling Guide

> Note: Recycling rules vary by municipality. Verify local acceptance
> with your waste hauler.

## Blue Bin (Recycle)

Accepted: - Paper - Newspapers and magazines - Flattened cardboard -
Cartons - Glass bottles and jars - Metal and tin cans - Aluminum
containers - Plastic containers (#1-#7 where accepted) - Bagged shredded
paper

Not accepted: - Plastic bags - Food waste - Food-soiled paper -
Disposable coffee cups - Candy wrappers - Batteries - Electronics -
Hazardous waste

## Organics

Accepted: - Food scraps - Bones - Napkins - Grass clippings - Leaves -
Yard trimmings - Sticks and twigs - Untreated wood

Do not compost treated or painted wood.

## Household Hazardous Waste

Take batteries, paint, chemicals, fluorescent bulbs, solvents, and
electronics to approved collection sites.

## Bulky Items

Arrange bulky item pickup when available. Donate usable furniture,
clothing, toys, and edible food.

## Recycling Tips

-   Empty and rinse containers.
-   Flatten cardboard.
-   Keep recyclables loose unless instructed otherwise.
-   Reduce waste by reusing and donating items.
